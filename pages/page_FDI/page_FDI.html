Dubai Investment Development Agency - DubaiFDI
DubaiFDI provides foreign investors and companies with advisory services on setup structure, location recommendation and necessary requirements and approvals for issuing a business license.
1- For setting up in mainland Dubai please dial 1
  • Caller’s Name
  • Company name if available (for existing company)
  • Business activity
  • Business structure (Branch of a foreign company, LLC)
  • Phone number
  • Email address
2- For setting up in a free zone please dial 2
  • Caller’s Name
  • Company name if available (for existing company)
  • Business activity
  • Business structure (Branch of a foreign company, LLC)
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Free zone of Choice
3. To follow up on pending requests please dial 3
4. For general information on Dubai Investment week please dial 4
  • Dubai Investment Week is an annual forum that provides investors with a comprehensive view of the investment opportunities, partnerships, innovation and the future of investment in all vital sectors that support the vision of Dubai and the UAE. For more information and updates, please visit
5. For general information please dial 5
  • Dubai FDI is located in Business Village, Deira, Bloc A, 9th floor. Our working hours are from 8am till 3pm from Sunday to Thursday. For more information, please visit our website
6. To speak to the operator please dial 0
